# 📊 Company Performance Overview Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Automobile Insurance Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze insurance company performance, premium generation, complaint trends, and market share distribution.

This dashboard provides insights into:

💰 Total Premium Written

🏆 Company Performance Ranking

📊 Complaint Ratio Analysis

🏢 Company-wise Premium Contribution

📈 Premium Trends & Distribution

📅 Year-wise Performance Analysis

The goal of this project is to help insurance stakeholders identify top-performing companies, monitor complaint patterns, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Insurance companies need to:

- Identify top-performing companies
- Analyze customer complaint trends
- Compare premium generation across competitors
- Monitor company rankings
- Improve customer satisfaction and service quality
- Support strategic business decisions

This dashboard helps answer these business questions effectively.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ KPI Cards

- Total Premium: 87.43K
- Total Ranking: 116K
- Total Companies: 1.358K
- Avg Complaint Ratio: 26.45

These KPIs provide a quick overview of industry performance.

---

### 2️⃣ Premium Analysis by Company

Visualizes premium contributions from major insurance companies including:

- GEICO General Insurance
- State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance
- Allstate Insurance Company
- GEICO Indemnity Company
- Government Employees Insurance Company

Helps identify leading companies in premium generation.

---

### 3️⃣ Complaint Analysis

Displays complaint distribution and complaint ratios by company.

Provides insights into:

- Customer satisfaction
- Service quality
- Complaint resolution performance

---

### 4️⃣ Market Share Distribution

Treemap and pie chart visualizations highlight:

- Company market share
- Premium concentration
- Competitive positioning

---

### 5️⃣ Year-wise Analysis

Interactive filtering by filing year enables trend analysis across different reporting periods.

---

### 6️⃣ Filters / Slicers

The dashboard includes dynamic filters for:

- Filing Year
- Complaint Analysis
- Premium Analysis
- Top Companies

Allows users to perform detailed and interactive analysis.
